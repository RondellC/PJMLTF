Fight to live

Is a song inspired by current events. It’s a song about overcoming a struggle. About reaching out to others while in isolation. It’s about finding new ways to live our daily lives. It’s about staying connected even though we’re apart.

It’s about doing our part to fight a silent war. To have faith that we can prevail even when circumstances seem to be at their worst. It’s about the darkest parts of the human condition and believing hope will prevail. We all need to play our part to fight these battles. And, working together we will win.

Musicians and artists alike are invited to participate in this project. #iplayedmypart speaks to our part in this battle and the part each musician and artist is welcome to play in this project. Let’s all play our part.

Together we will win.
